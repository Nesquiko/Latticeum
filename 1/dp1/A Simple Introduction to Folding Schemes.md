### Instance folding

Combines two computational statements S$_1$ , S$_2$ into one S$_3$. Thus S$_3$ implies correctness of S$_1$ and S$_2$.

### Constraint folding
Aggregates constraints of S$_1$ and S$_2$. S$_3$ satisfies all combined constraints.

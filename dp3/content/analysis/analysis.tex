\chapter{Analysis}\label{chap:analysis}

This chapter reviews the background needed for the proposed system. It first
summarizes Ethereum's consensus and execution model, then explains how
snarkification and ZkEVMs change block verification, and finally introduces the
lattice-based cryptographic primitives relevant to the design.

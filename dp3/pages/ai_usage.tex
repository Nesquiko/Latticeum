\chapter*{Use of AI}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Use of AI}
\markboth{Use of AI}{Use of AI}

AI was used in document writing, implementation, and evaluation.

\section{Document writing}

AI was used for proofreading, cross-checking citations, and drafting LaTeX commands
and TikZ diagrams.

This was confirmed by reading the generated corrections.
The cited papers were opened directly at the reported places, and the compiled
document and diagrams were checked visually.

\section{Implementation}

AI was used for debugging CCS satisfiability in SageMath, writing tests for correct
RISC-V VM execution and instruction decoding, and navigating the forked
dependencies.

This was confirmed by changing constraints until numbers in constraints matched
numbers from the Sage scripts, and until they actually passed. Running the
tests on compiled guest binaries, and opening the reported dependency sources to
verify that the guidance matched the code.

\section{Evaluation}

AI was used to research how other zkVMs are run.

This was confirmed by actually running the referenced systems and checking that
the described commands and observed behaviour matched.
